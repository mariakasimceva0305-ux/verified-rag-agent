# Incident Severity Classification

Severity levels:

- **SEV-1**: Full outage or severe security event affecting many users.
- **SEV-2**: Major degradation with significant business impact.
- **SEV-3**: Partial degradation with workaround available.
- **SEV-4**: Minor issue with low user impact.

Initial severity is assigned by Incident Manager and can be adjusted as evidence changes.

For SEV-1 and SEV-2 incidents, an incident channel and 30-minute update cadence are mandatory.
