# Response Time Guidelines

Target first response times:

- SEV-1: 5 minutes
- SEV-2: 15 minutes
- SEV-3: 4 business hours
- SEV-4: 1 business day

During business hours, Support owns initial response.
Outside business hours, On-call Operations handles SEV-1 and SEV-2.

Status updates are required every 30 minutes for SEV-1 and hourly for SEV-2.
