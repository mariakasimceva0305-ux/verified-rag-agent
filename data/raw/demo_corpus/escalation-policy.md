# Escalation Policy

Support tickets must be escalated when one of the following conditions is met:

- Business-critical service is unavailable for more than 15 minutes.
- Security impact is suspected.
- SLA breach is expected within the next 30 minutes.
- The issue remains unresolved after two ownership transfers.

Escalation path:
1. L1 Support -> L2 Operations
2. L2 Operations -> Incident Manager
3. Incident Manager -> Security Team (if security impact exists)

All escalations must include timeline, current impact, and mitigation status.
