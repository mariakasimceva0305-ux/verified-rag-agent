# Access Revocation Policy

Access revocation must be completed immediately for terminated employees and contractors.

- HR marks status as terminated in the HR system.
- IAM disables identity provider account.
- VPN, email, and source control access are revoked in the same workflow.

Target completion times:
- High-risk roles: within 15 minutes.
- All other roles: within 60 minutes.

Any revocation delay must be reported to Security Operations.
